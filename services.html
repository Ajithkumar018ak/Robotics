Advanced Offerings

Precision Engineering Services

We design, build, and deploy integrated automation hardware and algorithms. Our modules adapt to existing factory lines to minimize cycle times.

Industrial Automation

Complete assembly floor planning, mechanical design, sensor arrays configuration, and edge controllers setup. We optimize production setups to support high-throughput rates with minimal component waste.

  • Dynamic tooling and mechanical joint alignments
  • Low-latency controller networking protocols (EtherCAT, CANopen)
  • Custom control console dashboard integrations

Robotics Integration

Deploying heavy articulated robot arms, customized end-effectors, mobile loaders, and sorting systems. Our custom drive logic coordinates multi-joint paths to ensure smooth physical actions.

  • Articulated manipulators setup (ABB, Fanuc, Kuka style)
  • Custom payload gripper engineering
  • Collaborative work cell safety grid installations

Machine Vision & Anomaly Detection

Integrating industrial cameras, line scan setups, and edge GPU processing boards. Our visual segmentation pipelines inspect components for geometric and surface faults in real-time.

  • Sub-millimeter dimensional verification grids
  • Real-time surface anomaly detection classifiers
  • 3D bin picking vision alignment modules

Deployment Process Flow

How we audit, simulate, deploy, and verify our robotics platforms in client environments.

01

Audit & Analysis

We inspect physical workspace dimensions, identify line bottlenecks, and gather payload/cycle requirements.

02

Omni Simulation

03

System Integration
